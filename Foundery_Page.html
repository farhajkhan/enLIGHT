An Incubator Turning Students into Founders

How It Works

A cohort of hand-picked intercollegiate university students will go through a 4-month incubation program targeted at giving them the skills, knowledge, network, mindset, and experience they need to become successful entrepreneurs.

Do You Have What It Takes?

We’re looking for students that aren’t afraid to challenge the status quo, take action, and want to make an impact. Building out a business already or have startup ideas? Get 1-1 support to set your own milestones and goals. This program is for students who are serious about entrepreneurship and want to make their mark in the Canadian and global startup space.

Sound like you? enLIGHT is all about accessible education, so this program is absolutely free with no strings attached. Applications are open to students from any post-secondary institution across Ontario, any year and any faculty! This program is made by students, for students and is tailored for the busy schedules and lives of student founders.
